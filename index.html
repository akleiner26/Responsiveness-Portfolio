About Me

Andrew Kleiner is a professional lighting designer living in the greater Philadelphia area. With almost a decade of experience, he has worked for bands such as The Head and the Heart, Ani Difranco, and San Fermin. Outside of touring, he has done large scale events such as the NBA All Star Game, and the NHL All Star Game. Andrew havs a Bachelor's Degree in Communications with a minor in music composition from Tulane University.
